WWE's Monday Night Raw Debuts on Netflix: John Cena and The Rock Return in a Historic Event

WWE made history on January 6, 2025, with the debut of Monday Night Raw on Netflix, broadcasting live from the futuristic Intuit Dome in Inglewood, California. The show featured the electrifying returns of John Cena and Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson, thrilling fans worldwide and adding star power to this groundbreaking event. The Intuit Dome, a $2 billion arena, provided a spectacular setting, boasting advanced features like the world’s largest 4K Halo Board and the iconic 51-row Wall. The event included a high-stakes main event match between CM Punk and Seth Rollins, while Roman Reigns teased a potential WrestleMania 41 dream match. This Netflix partnership marks a transformative era for WWE, expanding its reach to a global streaming audience. The successful debut combined legendary appearances, cutting-edge technology, and compelling storylines, setting a new standard for live wrestling entertainment in the digital age.
