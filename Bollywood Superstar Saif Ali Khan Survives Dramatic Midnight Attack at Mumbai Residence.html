Bollywood Superstar Saif Ali Khan Survives Dramatic Midnight Attack at Mumbai Residence

In a shocking turn of events, Bollywood royalty Saif Ali Khan narrowly escaped a fatal encounter when an unidentified assailant infiltrated his luxurious Bandra home in Mumbai during the early hours of January 16, 2025. The chilling incident unfolded at 2:30 AM, leaving the 54-year-old actor with six stab wounds, two of which dangerously grazed his spine. Swift medical intervention at Lilavati Hospital saved the actor, with doctors confirming his vital organs remain unharmed. Adding to the drama, Saif's wife, Kareena Kapoor, and their two young children were present in the home but miraculously escaped harm. Authorities suspect the attack was a high-stakes burglary gone wrong, plunging the city into a frenzy. The Mumbai Police have launched a massive manhunt to catch the culprit, as fans across the globe flood social media with prayers and support for the "Nawab of Bollywood."
